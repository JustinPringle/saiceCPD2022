This course aims to develop and demonstrate innovation, technical competence and professional expertise in Coastal and Water Engineering. We present the latest international trends in engineering design and knowledge within the context of climate change. This course combines new concepts in design with practical applications using UKZN’s state of the art research laboratories. Participants will have the chance to run a wave flume to visualise and interpret how waves interact with coastal protection schemes.
